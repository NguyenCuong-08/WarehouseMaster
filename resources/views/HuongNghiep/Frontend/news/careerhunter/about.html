About Us

We’re more than just
a career-matching platform.
We’re CareerHunter.

Our Mission

It’s simple. We want to help you find the career that you’ll love, just as importantly, excel in.

Our Journey

CareerHunter was launched in 2017 to support students, graduates and professionals in their quest for the perfect career, and was developed using the minds and knowledge of a highly experienced team of psychologists and psychometricians, as well as our career experts over at CareerAddict.

Since then, our career testing platform has matched thousands of people to careers that accurately fit their interests, personality, goals and skills through our series of psychometric self-assessments.

Unrivaled Testing

  • Career compatibility with accuracy

    CareerHunter’s unique algorithm will evaluate your test results and accurately match you to suitable careers that you’ll not only love but also succeed in.

  • Smart psychometric analysis

    Delve deep into your psychometric makeup with analysis of over 70 dimensions to fully grasp where your strengths and weaknesses lie.

  • Intuitive and personalized insights
