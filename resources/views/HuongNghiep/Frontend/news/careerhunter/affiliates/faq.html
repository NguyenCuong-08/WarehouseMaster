Settings and account
  • How can I apply to become a CareerHunter™ affiliate?

    You can apply to join our affiliate programme by completing the application form on the Affiliates page. We will then review your application based on our selection criteria and be in touch with you within 1–2 business days.

  • Are there any fees to register as an affiliate?

    No. The CareerHunter™ affiliate programme is free of charge.

  • Can I apply to the CareerHunter™ affiliate programme if I don’t have a website?

    Sure! While having a website is a great way to get traffic, it’s not strictly necessary to join our affiliate programme. If you’re accepted into the programme, you can still promote CareerHunter™ on forums, your social media accounts and any other allowed means.

  • What are your selection criteria for affiliate applications?

    We look at a variety of factors when considering affiliate applications, including:

    • Site traffic
    • Social media presence
    • Site content
    • Relevance and alignment to our brand

    Applicants must be at least 18 years of age and provide a legitimate name.

  • Why was my application rejected?

    Your application could have been rejected due to multiple reasons, including but not limited to:

    • Your website has low traffic and/or your social media accounts have low engagement
    • Your target audience is under 13 years of age
    • Your content promotes sexually explicit materials, violence, discrimination and/or illegal activities, and/or violates intellectual property rights
    • Your website is still under construction
    • Your services/products or audience are not a great fit for CareerHunter

    We make every effort to fully explain the reasons for your application’s rejection in our response.

  • Do I have to join the affiliate programme on a CPA basis or can I earn commissions from other referral mediums?

    In addition to earning commissions through our main CPA model, which allows you to create and share affiliate links and coupons as well as monitor your performance through a personal dashboard, you can also sign up as a Referrer. As a Referrer, you promote CareerHunter™ on mediums like your YouTube channel or blog and receive a fixed flat fee for your advertising efforts.

  • How can I change my account email/password?

    You can change your email address and password in your Settings area while logged into your CareerHunter™ affiliate account. Simply click on ‘Change email’ or ‘Change password’ and follow the on-screen instructions. You can also change other account details here, like your name and phone number.

  • How can I control the email notifications I receive from you?

    To control what email notifications you receive from us, simply navigate to the ‘Settings’ page while logged into your CareerHunter™ affiliate account. In the ‘Email notifications’ area, select or deselect which notifications you’d like to receive going forward, and click ‘Save’.

  • What is my affiliate ID?

    Your unique affiliate ID is the ID associated with your account and is automatically embedded in the affiliate links you create and share to your audience. This helps us track and attribute sales to you that come from your advertising channels. Your affiliate ID is displayed throughout your account near the top of each page.

  • Why was my account suspended?

    Your account may have been suspended due to multiple reasons, without prior notice, including your content and/or promotional methods violate our rules of promotion, and/or there was missing or misleading information in your affiliate application

  • I would like to delete my affiliate account. How can I do that?

    If you would like to delete your CareerHunter™ affiliate account, please email test at careeraddict dot com, and a member of our support team will help you do so.

    Please note that deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. If you later change your mind, you won’t be able to restore your account and will need to complete the application process again.

Payments and earnings
  • What is considered a sale?

    You will earn a commission from every referral you send to CareerHunter™ through your unique affiliate link and that purchases full access to the CareerHunter™ testing platform via the ‘Upgrade’ page.

  • How much commission can I earn?

    This depends on a variety of factors, including your channels’ traffic as well as the number of credible referrals you can deliver to CareerHunter™. Commission rates vary between 20% and 50% of the purchase price, and are agreed upon during the application process. If your channels’ audience grows over time, you may be eligible for a higher commission than the initially agreed rate.

  • How will I get paid?

    You will need to manually request payment of your commissions through the Payments section of our affiliate account. Payments are currently issued via PayPal.

  • When will I get paid?

    You can request a payout once your account reaches the minimum payout amount. We will then review your request and release the funds to your PayPal account within 30 days. Please note that it may take up to an additional five business days for funds to appear in your PayPal account.

  • How often can I request a payout?

    You can request a payout as often as you like, provided you have reached the minimum payout amount each time.

  • What is the minimum payout amount?

    Yes. The minimum payout amount is USD/EUR/GBP100.

  • What currency will I be paid in?

    Earnings are paid out in USD, EUR or GBP, depending on the currency designated to your affiliate account upon setup.

  • Can I earn commissions on my own purchases?

    No. Your affiliate account will not be credited with commissions when you make a purchase using your own affiliate link. All earnings which will be attributed to your affiliate account must come from your customers/audience.

  • Do I need to pay taxes on my earnings?

    Please consult your accountant on whether you need to declare the commissions you earn from the CareerHunter™ affiliate programme. We do not offer tax-related guidelines, consultations or services.

Promotion and tracking
  • How long does a tracking cookie remain in a user’s browser?

    We know that customers don’t always make a purchase right away, so we offer a 30-day cookie window, meaning if a referral leaves CareerHunter™ and later returns to make a purchase during that window, you will earn a commission on that purchase. If, however, a referral returns to the website and makes a purchase after the conclusion of the 30-day window, no commission will be credited to your account.

  • Can I see reports of my referred sales and commissions?

    Yes, you view detailed information and graphics about your sales and commissions, as well as clicks and conversion rates, through your personal dashboard while logged into your CareerHunter™ affiliate account.

  • Can I promote CareerHunter™ on other channels, like social media?

    Yes! You can promote CareerHunter™ with your generated affiliate links anywhere you want, including on your social media accounts, on forums and even in email blasts, subject to the rules of promotion.

  • What are the rules or guidelines for promoting CareerHunter™?

    Please read the rules of promotion of the CareerHunter™ affiliate programme here.
