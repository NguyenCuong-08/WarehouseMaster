Earnings

Affiliate earnings are paid via PayPal to the account that is provided inside your CareerHunter™ account area. No cash or other alternative payments are currently available.

Earnings from valid sales can be monitored inside the Affiliate account area. Earnings are locked for 30 days and then are made available for withdrawal via the form of a payment request.

The Affiliate must request a payout via the Affiliate account area, provided that the Affiliate has reached the minimum payout amount of 100.00 US dollars/Euro/Pound sterling. No automatic payments will be issued at any given time.

Payment requests will be reviewed internally and processed within 5 working days from the request date. We reserve the right to withhold earnings if we have evidence or reason to believe that such earnings were accumulated through fraudulent means and/or by otherwise violating these Rules of Promotion and/or our Terms of Use

In the event a customer requests a refund for a transaction for which the Affiliate has earned commissions, any commissions earned on the refund amount will be deducted from the Affiliate’s balance.

CareerHunter™ reserves the right to change commission rates, processing procedures and/or payment schedules at any time and for any reason without prior notice.
